Tools

Adobe CC

Expert ability with a wide range of Adobe utilities including: Photoshop, Illustrator, Audition, Sound Booth, Premiere, and After Effects.

Clickteam Fusion

High-level LUA platform that allows for custom-built games that can be ported to HTML, Android, PC, and Mac/iOs.

Audacity

A simple, yet powerful audio editing program that allows for quick and easy edits. I use it primarily for creating loops from tracks.

Unity

A game development platform that recently gained VR support, a field I am expanding my game development to.

Web Development

MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node.JS are used in my current frameworks, and were used to build this site.

GitBash

Clone, push, pull, and create/manage branches from the command line interface. It's quicker than using the UI.

Autodesk Inventor

Conceptualize, model, and map dimensions for small-scale building projects for everyday life and home renovations.

OBS Studio

Open Broadcaster Software; Powerful recording software I use for video streaming or general purpose tutorial recording.

Microsoft Office

I use Excel on a heavy basis to keep track of personal finances and databases.
